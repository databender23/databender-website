**Target:** Law firms, 20-75+ attorneys
**Key Pain Points:**

- Knowledge walking out when partners retire
- 15+ software systems that don't talk to each other
- Client relationships trapped in inboxes

**Solutions:** Knowledge Management, Client Intelligence, Business Development, Associate Productivity, Matter Analytics, Integration Hub

**Lead Magnets (Free Guides):**
