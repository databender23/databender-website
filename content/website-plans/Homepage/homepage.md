## Design Notes

- Hero should be clean, confident, not cluttered
- Use imagery that suggests business outcomes, not technology
- Avoid stock photos of "data visualization" or "people pointing at screens"
- Color palette should convey trust and competence
- Mobile-first design—many visitors will be on phones

## Content Priority

If space is limited, prioritize in this order:
1. Hero (positioning + CTA)
2. What We Do (three capabilities)
3. Assessment CTA
4. Industries
5. Why Databender
6. Results/Testimonials
